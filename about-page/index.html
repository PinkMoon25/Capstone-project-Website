Learn Web Development

devHome is an open-source learning platform for things related to Web.
Here at devHome we believe everybody has a right to learn and educate themselves, it is also paramount for growth and development and ultimately a better life.
We work towards sharing information & knowledge to make it availaible to everyone, everywhere.
We have curated thousands of content from various resources to provide anybody with the hunger to learn and master web-development.
Our program incorporates project based-learning, and you can learn anytime, anywhere.
All it's gonna take is your time and effort.
